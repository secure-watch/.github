# Code of Conduct

[한국어](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.ko.md)

## Our standards

Secure Watch contributors are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and constructively
- focus on the work and its technical context
- welcome different perspectives and backgrounds
- accept evidence-based feedback
- protect private and security-sensitive information

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disclosure of private information, and disruptive conduct.

## Enforcement

Use GitHub's [Report content](https://docs.github.com/en/communities/maintaining-your-safety-on-github/reporting-abuse-or-spam) option on an Issue, pull request, Discussion, or comment to report conduct concerns to repository maintainers. If that option is unavailable or the concern is not tied to specific content, report it to GitHub Support. Do not disclose sensitive details in public Issues or Discussions.

Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or take other action they consider necessary to keep the community safe and productive.
